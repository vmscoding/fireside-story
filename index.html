It was a dark and stormy night...

When the group pulled into the old campground. It wasn't quite as bright and welcoming-looking as they remembered. The old sign that had once greeted their arrival to summer camp now...

...Now hung by a single nail, creaking ominously in the breeze. A black cloud drifted in front of the sun. In the depths of their souls, the group felt the spectre of death pass them by.

Albert: Well, heck.

Joey: This isn't nearly as great as I remember. Do you guys wanna go to Taco Bell instead?

Their spirits not yet sated by the desolate vista that lay before them, the group set their sights on a plae once compared to the firey depths of Malbolge - the Taco Bell drive-thru.
